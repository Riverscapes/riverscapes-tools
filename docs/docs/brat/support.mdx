Questions about BRAT can be submitted to [support@riverscapes.freshdesk.com](mailto:support@riverscapes.freshdesk.com). There are also several [beaver related communities](https://www.riverscapes.net/topics) on the Riverscapes community platform where you can connect with others with similar interests.

BRAT data can be discovered, viewed and downloaded at the [Riverscapes Data Exchange](https://data.riverscapes.net/s?type=Project&projectTypeId=riverscapes_brat&view=map).
